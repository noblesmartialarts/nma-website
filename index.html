Meet Sensei Brandon Noble

Hi, I'm Sensei Brandon Noble — with 30+ years in martial arts and a lifelong passion for sharing the gift with the next generation. My journey started in 1991 as a three-year-old, mesmerized by the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

Over my career I've trained 5,000+ students, promoted 400+ black belts, helped open 3 martial arts schools, and led a demo team that performed nationwide.

Proud dad to Brayden, Cameron, and Kayley, and a transplant from Sacramento, CA to Wake Forest, NC. My goal is simple: inspire, teach, and make a positive impact — one student at a time.

Good to Know

Parent FAQ

Where do I start?

New students ages 4–6 start in Little Dragons. Ages 7–13 start in Karate Kids. Both are beginner-friendly — no experience needed.

How does registration work?

Registration is through Wake Forest Parks & Recreation (RecDesk). Sessions run monthly. Sibling discounts are applied automatically at checkout.

What should my child wear?

T-shirt and athletic pants are perfect for the first class. NMA uniforms and shirts are available to order in the Parent Hub.

How does belt progression work?

Each 2-month cycle: Stripe 1 (month 1), Stripe 2 (month 2), then Belt Test at end of month 2. $10 belt fee covers the belt. Promotions are based on effort, attitude, and consistency — not perfection.

Where are classes held?

Wake Forest Community House, 133 W. Owen Ave, Wake Forest, NC 27587. All classes are on Mondays. Open in Maps →

Belt System

The Ranking Journey

🐉 Little Dragons Belt Path

LD belts are white with a colored stripe through the center. After LD Black Belt, students advance to Karate Kids with a Yellow II or Orange II belt — a solid belt with a black stripe honoring their LD achievement.

🥋 Karate Kids Belt Path

After Orange/Orange II, all students promote to Purple Belt and enter the Black Belt Club.

⚔️ Black Belt Club Path

🏆 Master's Club Path

Culminates in a multi-week Black Belt Boot Camp and multi-day examination.

🌟 Legends Club

Black belt is the beginning of mastery. Minimum journey: ~36 months.

Parent Playbook

✅ Practice at Home

2–3 times per week, 10–15 minutes using the practice videos. Consistency beats perfection every time.

✅ Reinforce at Home

We're building black belt behavior, not just skills. Encourage respect, listening, effort, and a positive attitude at home.

✅ Understand Promotions

Promotions are based on effort, attitude, consistency, and growth. Before each belt test you'll receive an Intent to Promote letter. You decide if your child is demonstrating black belt excellence at home.
